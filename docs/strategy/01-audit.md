# 01 — Competitor Audit: web.barq.agency

*Phase 1 deliverable. Source: public pages crawled July 2026 — home, services, pricing, about, portfolio, and the AI-service pages.*

## 1. Snapshot

**barq** is a Hebrew-language digital agency serving small-to-medium businesses in Israel. Its pitch: fast, designed, "automated" websites with **no setup fee** on a **convenient monthly subscription**, plus a broad menu of marketing and AI add-ons. The name means "lightning" (Arabic *barq*) — speed, power, creativity.

| Attribute | Value |
|---|---|
| Market | Israeli SMBs |
| Language | Hebrew only (one About line in English) |
| Model | Monthly subscription, no setup fee; hosting/domain/SSL bundled |
| Positioning | "We don't just build websites — we build digital experiences that drive businesses forward" |
| Contact | Phone, WhatsApp, email, "free consultation"; Facebook / Instagram / LinkedIn |
| Trust stats | 150+ projects · 120+ clients · 5+ years · 98% satisfaction · 24/7 support |

## 3. Service taxonomy (21 services, 5 groups)

- **Web design & development** — web design, web development, e-commerce, landing pages, corporate sites, redesign.
- **Digital marketing** — SEO, PPC, social media, WhatsApp marketing, email marketing, Google Business.
- **Content & branding** — copywriting, logo & branding, product photography, graphic design, blog writing.
- **UX/UI design** — UX/UI.
- **AI solutions** — AI chatbot (24/7 service, lead capture), business automation (emails, reminders, lead management, integrations, time savings), custom AI tools (recommendation systems, data analysis, smart CRM).

**Process** (stated): 4 steps — consultation → planning/design → development → launch & support.

## 4. Pricing model (the sharpest idea)

Monthly subscription, **no setup fee**; all plans include hosting, domain, SSL. Support duration scales with tier.

| Tier | Price/mo | Core inclusions |
|---|---|---|
| Barq Mini | ₪89 | Up to 3 pages, contact form, WhatsApp, basic SEO, CMS, blog, 1-mo support |
| Barq Lite | ₪189 | Up to 5 pages, advanced forms, Google Business, 3-mo support |
| Barq Professional ⭐ | ₪275 | Up to 10 pages, advanced SEO, ~20 posts, 6-mo support |
| Barq Golden | ₪585 | Unlimited pages, reporting, unlimited support, e-commerce (≤50 products), AI chatbot |

**Add-ons:** maintenance ₪500/mo · content ₪300/page · photography from ₪1,500 · ad campaign from ₪2,000. Standard sites ship in 4–6 weeks; training + file handover on delivery.

## 5. Messaging analysis

- **Tone:** reassuring, SMB-friendly, benefit-led but generic. Heavy on "professional / fair / full support / measurable results."
- **Value props:** professional team, transparent pricing, full support, measurable results.
- **Proof:** relies on round-number stats and unnamed testimonials; the About team names read as placeholders.
- **CTAs:** "Let's start," "Contact now," "Free consultation," "Start your project."

## 6. What barq does well (worth learning from)

1. **Recurring-revenue model** — no-setup-fee subscription lowers the buying barrier and creates predictable value. This is their single best idea.
2. **Breadth of menu** — a one-stop shop, including **AI chatbots, automation, and custom AI** as first-class services (on-trend, high-margin).
3. **Pricing transparency** — a dedicated pricing page with clear tiers reduces friction.
4. **Industry framing** — portfolio filterable by vertical helps visitors self-identify.
5. **Bundled essentials** — hosting/domain/SSL included removes decision fatigue.
6. **Low-commitment CTA** — "free consultation" as the entry point.

## 7. Weaknesses / gaps (openings for Devora)

| Weakness | Opening for Devora |
|---|---|
| Generic value-prop language ("professional," "fair") | Outcome-led, specific, editorial voice |
| Portfolio has no depth — no results, no detail pages | Case-study **detail pages**: challenge → approach → outcome |
| Placeholder-looking team, unnamed testimonials | Keep proof **honest**; don't fabricate |
| Single language (Hebrew) | **Bilingual EN/AR**, MENA + worldwide reach |
| Undifferentiated ("SMB + subscription") | Sharp "one team, end to end — dev + aura" |
| Thin AI pages (feature lists, no depth) | Position AI/automation as an engineering-led capability |
| No clear process narrative | A calm, 5-step **Process** page |

## 8. Strengths / Weaknesses / Opportunities (Devora read)

- **Strengths to press:** senior one-team model, bilingual craft, real live client sites, editorial design system, engineering depth (the "dev" in dev+aura).
- **Weaknesses to fix:** the pre-existing site had placeholder pricing/testimonials, no About/Process/Pricing/Industries/FAQ, no case depth, no AI offering.
- **Opportunities adopted:** AI & Automation discipline · productized Care Plan (recurring) · dedicated transparent pricing · light industry targeting · a low-friction "book a call" CTA.
