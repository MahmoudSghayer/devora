# 03 — Content Transformation

*Phase 2 deliverable. Section by section: existing purpose → improved version → new copy → better headline → better CTA. Original ideas only; nothing copied from barq.*

Format per section: **Purpose** · **What improved** · **Headline** · **CTA**.

---

## Home — hero
- **Purpose:** state what Devora is in one breath.
- **Improved:** sharper "one senior team" claim; the dev+aura idea made explicit; outcome-led.
- **Headline:** *"We design, build & launch websites. End to end."*
- **Sub:** "Strategy, brand, design, code and growth — one senior team from first call to launch day. Dev meets aura: websites with a presence you can feel."
- **CTA:** primary *Start a project* · secondary *See the work*.

## Home — services overview
- **Improved:** added a **5th discipline, AI & Automation**, so the "everything under one roof" claim now includes the on-trend, engineering-led capability barq leads with — but framed as craft, not a feature dump.
- **Headline:** *"Everything a website needs. Under one roof."*

## Home — new teasers (added)
- **Process teaser** — *"A clear path from brief to launch."* → links to `/process`.
- **Industries teaser** — *"Made for teams that live by their website."* → links to `/industries`.
- **Care Plan teaser** — *"We don't disappear at launch."* → links to `/pricing` (the recurring-revenue idea, reframed premium).
- **FAQ teaser** — *"Good questions, answered."* → links to `/faq`.

## Services
- **Purpose:** show the full capability and how to engage.
- **Improved:** four disciplines → **five** (adds AI & Automation); replaced the old pricing block with an **engagement-models** section (Project / Care Plan / Embedded team) that routes to the new pricing page.
- **Headline:** *"Five disciplines. One pipeline."*
- **CTA:** *Compare & see pricing* → `/pricing`.

## Work + case studies
- **Purpose:** prove capability with real launches.
- **Improved:** barq's portfolio has no depth; Devora adds **case-study detail pages** (`/work/[slug]`) with **challenge → approach → outcome**, kept honest and qualitative. Listing rows gain a *View case study* button.
- **Headline:** *"Real sites, real launches."*

## Pricing (new)
- **Purpose:** remove price friction, present the recurring option.
- **Improved:** adopts barq's transparency, at a premium altitude. Two project tiers (**Launch**, **Studio**) + a monthly **Care Plan**; an "every engagement includes" strip (the bilingual-ready line is a differentiator barq can't claim); a focused pricing FAQ.
- **Headline:** *"Transparent from the first email."*
- **Honesty:** "Placeholder figures — every project gets a custom quote."

## About (new)
- **Purpose:** make the "one team, senior" claim believable.
- **Improved:** a real narrative (dev + aura), four crisp differentiators, four values, a crew block.
- **Headline:** *"One team with a bias for shipping."*

## Process (new)
- **Improved:** barq's 4 generic steps → Devora's calm, 5-move path with **"what you get"** at each stage (Discover, Define, Design, Build, Launch & grow).
- **Headline:** *"From brief to live in five moves."*

## Industries (new)
- **Improved:** barq filters a portfolio by vertical; Devora states **who it builds for** and the tailored value per vertical — without inventing case counts.
- **Headline:** *"Built for teams that live by their website."*

## FAQ (new)
- **Improved:** grouped, honest answers (General · Pricing & engagement · Working together · Ownership & tech). Doubles as SEO surface and objection handling.
- **Headline:** *"Questions, answered."*

## Contact
- **Improved:** added a low-friction **"book a call"** block ("no pitch, just your project") — a premium take on barq's "free consultation."
- **Headline:** *"Let's build yours."*

---

### Removed / avoided (anti-patterns from barq)
- Generic "professional / fair / full support" value props.
- Invented stats and unnamed testimonials.
- A 21-item thin service list — Devora stays **curated**.
- Feature-dump AI pages — reframed as engineering-led outcomes.
